Built with Realism and Usability in Mind

Titanium CAD was built to be as realistic as possible, while still maintaining the usability and simplicity of most other CADs. To accomplish this, we use a clean dark-theme for the pages typical users will visit, and use a more realistic and feature-rich page for dispatchers and emergency services units.

CAD

Titanium gives you the ability to manage everything in your community. With realtime updates, our CAD system allows dispatchers to manage units, calls, bolos, and so much more.

Affordable

Our pricing system allows you to pay for what you really need, instead of focusing on arbitrary factors like the total number of civilians in your database.

Simple Permissions

Giving users permissions in the CAD is as easy as selecting the user and selecting the permissions they need, and it looks pretty nice too!

Civilians/Vehicles/Firearms

Register civilians, vehicles, and firearms in the database with ease! Simply enter the required information and any officer will be able to look it up!
